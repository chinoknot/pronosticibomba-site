Privacy Policy – Pronostici Bomba Last updated: 11 December 2025 Pronostici Bomba ("the App") is developed and provided as a free informational tool offering statistical football predictions, analysis, and daily picks. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user data. 1. No Personal Data Collected The App does not collect, store, or process: Personal information Names, emails, phone numbers Location or GPS data Contacts, photos, media, or files Device identifiers Advertising IDs The App does not require an account or login of any kind. 2. Data Processing The App retrieves football match data, statistics, and odds from external APIs. This information is not connected to the user and contains no personal identifiers. 3. Third-Party Services The App may use: Supabase (for storing model data and picks) API-Football (for football statistics and fixtures) These services handle only match data. No personal user information is ever sent to these services. 4. Cookies and Tracking The App: does not use cookies does not implement analytics does not track user behavior does not show personalized advertising 5. Children’s Privacy The App is not directed to children under 13, and it does not collect any information that would require parental consent under COPPA or similar laws. 6. Security We do not collect personal data; however, best practices are applied to ensure that all server-side services store only aggregated football-related information, never user-related data. 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at the URL containing this document. 8.
