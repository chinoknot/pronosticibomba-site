Privacy Policy – Pronostici Bomba

Last updated: 11 December 2025

Pronostici Bomba ("the App") is provided as a free informational tool offering statistical football predictions, analysis, and daily picks.

1. No Personal Data Collected

The App does not collect, store, or process any personal information. It does not gather names, emails, phone numbers, device identifiers, or location data.

2. Data Processing

The App retrieves football match data, statistics, and odds from external APIs. No personal user data is ever transmitted.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use Supabase and API-Football to fetch and store football-related data. These services do not receive any user-related information.

4. Cookies and Tracking

The App does not use cookies, analytics, tracking scripts, or personalized advertising.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not collect information requiring parental consent.

6. Security

While no user data is collected, best practices are applied to secure our infrastructure.
